Question.

At TEDxNYUAD we believe in opening new horizons and broadening our perspectives. As we dream to inspire people to make an impact, this year we have assembled a host of speakers who question how we view the world. We want to take our audience on a journey of the unconventional.

For TEDxNYUAD 2015 we will be exploring the theme Question. Our speakers will be sharing their untold stories as they redefine the concepts of home and memory, provide a fresh look on traditions and videogaming, change the way we see – or don’t see – chess, and show the true power of youth.
